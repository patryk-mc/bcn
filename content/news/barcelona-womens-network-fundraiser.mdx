Lyn Lozano Galicia joined the **Barcelona Women's Network Arts & Experiences Fundraiser** for an inspiring evening of art, connection, and community.

The event helped raise vital funds for **Casal dels Infants** and its work supporting children and families in vulnerable situations across the city.

Evenings like this are a reminder of why we love being part of Barcelona's international community, not just as a service, but as neighbours who show up for the people and causes that make this city what it is.
