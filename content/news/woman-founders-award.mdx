We are proud to share that our founder, **Lyn Lozano Galicia**, received the Woman Founder's Award at this year's Impulse Awards.

The recognition celebrates the women building businesses with purpose, and it means a great deal to a team that started just after the pandemic with a simple mission: to make life easier for Barcelona's international community through reliable, trusted home services.

This award belongs to the whole team. Thank you to everyone who has trusted us with their homes over the years, and to the families and colleagues who continue to make this work so rewarding.
